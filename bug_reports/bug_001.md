# Bug Report — BUG-001

## Title
Negative quantity can be added to cart

## Environment
- Browser: Chrome
- OS: Windows 11
- URL: https://automationexercise.com

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Go to https://automationexercise.com/products
2. Click on any product
3. In the Quantity field enter -11111
4. Click "Add to cart"

## Expected Result
System should show an error: "Quantity must be a positive number"

## Actual Result
Product is added to cart with negative quantity (-11111)

## Severity
Medium

## Priority
High
